Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeoClip.

Where is my clipboard data stored?

Directly on your computer! We do not have servers to store your data. If you upgrade to Pro and turn on sync, your data is backed up to a secure, private folder in your own Google Drive.

Can I use my Pro license on multiple computers?

Yes! Your Pro license is tied to your Google Drive account. As long as you connect the same Google Drive on your computers, your Pro features and history will automatically sync.

What happens if my Pro subscription expires?

Your saved data will never be deleted. You can still view everything you've copied. New saves will simply pause once you reach the free limit (50 items and 3 collections) until you renew.

How do I copy text from images?

We integrate directly with Google Lens for fast text extraction and visual search.

When you copy an image, open NeoClip and click the Google Lens button inside the card. It will open an overlay allowing you to select, translate, or copy text directly from the image.

Why did it miss a copied item?

Browsers have strict security rules that prevent extensions from spying on your clipboard in the background while you are working in other apps. If you copy multiple items outside your browser (e.g., in Word, Photoshop, or Excel), only the last copied item is captured when you focus back on your browser.

The Solution: Open the NeoClip panel by pressing Alt+V (default shortcut) or clicking the floating bubble, then press Win+V (on Windows) to open your OS clipboard history and paste the items you missed directly. NeoClip will capture them instantly.

How to customize the shortcut: You can change the default Alt+V (or Option+V on Mac) shortcut by visiting:
chrome://extensions/shortcuts (or edge://extensions/shortcuts in Microsoft Edge).

What is the default shortcut to open NeoClip, and how do I change it?

By default, NeoClip is configured to open with the shortcut Alt+V (or Option+V on Mac) across all browsers (including Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Cốc Cốc).

How to customize your shortcut:
• Chrome / Brave / Cốc Cốc: Visit chrome://extensions/shortcuts
• Microsoft Edge: Visit edge://extensions/shortcuts
Find NeoClip and click inside the box next to "Toggle Magic Clip Panel" to assign your preferred key combination.

Does Auto-Paste work everywhere?

Most websites support it! When you click an item in NeoClip, it is automatically pasted for you. If a highly secure website blocks this, you can always paste manually using Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac).

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Check your email for the receipt sent by Lemon Squeezy when you upgraded. It contains a Manage Subscription link leading to your Customer Portal where you can manage payments or cancel anytime.
